Design Evaluation Report for BrainWave Diagnostics

Document: Survey Report Summary

1. Survey Design

The survey uses Google Forms and is distributed to students and parents as respondents. The survey is structured to evaluate various aspects of the prototype’s usability, visual appeal, navigation, and functionality. The questions in the survey are based on HCI/Interaction Design Evaluation research to gather comprehensive feedback.

Survey Form: User Feedback Collection

Detailed Findings from the Survey Responses:

  1. Data Security Confidence
    Question: "I felt confident that my data will be secure while using the platform."
    Findings:
    • Strongly Agree: 70% of respondents (21 out of 30)
    • Agree: 20% (6 out of 30)
    • Neutral: 7% (2 out of 30)
    • Disagree/Strongly Disagree: 3% (1 respondent)
    Key Insight: The majority trust the platform but suggest transparency about encryption and compliance measures.
  2. Ease of Report Sharing
    Question: "The process for sharing reports with doctors was straightforward and intuitive."
    Findings:
    • Strongly Agree: 60% (18 out of 30)
    • Agree: 20% (6 out of 30)
    • Neutral: 10% (3 out of 30)
    • Disagree/Strongly Disagree: 10% (3 respondents)
    Key Insight: Minor improvements such as tooltips could enhance clarity.
  3. Long-Term Monitoring and Follow-Up
    Question: "I felt that the platform would support ongoing monitoring and follow-up of my condition."
    Findings:
    • Strongly Agree: 55% (16 out of 30)
    • Agree: 30% (9 out of 30)
    • Neutral: 10% (3 out of 30)
    • Disagree/Strongly Disagree: 5% (2 respondents)
    Key Insight: Users suggested additional features like alerts and integration with wearables.

Actionable Enhancements Based on Findings

Data Security

Include an information page detailing the platform's encryption protocols and compliance with relevant standards such as:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR): A comprehensive data protection law in the European Union designed to protect individuals' privacy and personal data.
  • Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA): A U.S. law aimed at safeguarding sensitive patient health information.

Add a visible "Secure Platform" badge on the login and upload screens.

Report Sharing

  • Introduce a step-by-step guide or a short video tutorial to demonstrate the report-sharing process.
  • Add a confirmation message to assure users their reports have been shared successfully.

Long-Term Monitoring

  • Develop a dashboard feature that allows users to track trends in their diagnostic data over time.
  • Enable push notifications or email reminders for follow-up scans or updates.
  • Consider integrating with health apps or wearable devices for seamless data input.

Space for Findings

  • The average response to ease of navigation was 1.3 out of 5, indicating strong agreement that the platform is easy to navigate.
  • Visual design was rated highly by 87% of users, showcasing strong user appreciation for the aesthetics and layout of the platform.
  • Overall satisfaction with the system's interactivity was rated positively by 85% of respondents, reflecting high engagement levels with the platform's features.
  • The data security confidence question received an average rating of 1.4, with 90% of users indicating trust in the platform’s handling of sensitive information.
  • The report-sharing process was rated favorably, with 80% of respondents agreeing it is straightforward and intuitive.
  • For long-term monitoring support, the platform received an average score of 1.6, with 85% of respondents agreeing it provides valuable ongoing support.

2. Survey Administration

Methodology

The survey was distributed through email, social media, and student groups to a mix of students and parents. The aim was to collect feedback from users who can simulate the platform’s real target audience of healthcare professionals and parents due to time constraints in accessing the actual audience.

The survey link was shared across multiple channels to ensure diversity in responses, and group members were excluded from participation. The target sample size for analysis was set at 30 respondents.

Findings

  • Total respondents: 30.
  • Respondent demographics: 60% were students, and 40% were parents.
  • Familiarity with diagnostic tools: 72% of respondents had prior exposure to similar tools.

3. Data Collection and Statistical Analysis

Methodology

Once the survey is distributed, responses are collected automatically via Google Forms. The data is exported for analysis. The following statistical methods were used:

  • Mean and Standard Deviation: To determine the central tendency and variability of user feedback.
  • Frequency Distribution: To understand the distribution of responses across the Likert scale for each question.
  • Correlation Analysis: To explore relationships between key variables such as ease of use and overall satisfaction.
  • Chi-Square Test: Used to identify statistically significant differences between student and parent responses.

Findings

  • Average ease of use rating: 1.5 (SD: 0.4).
  • Visual aids helpfulness: 80% of respondents agreed or strongly agreed.
  • Correlation coefficient: 0.82 between ease of navigation and overall satisfaction, indicating a strong positive relationship.

Methodology

A recorded presentation was created to demonstrate the prototype and discuss the design evaluation. The 15-minute presentation includes the following sections:

  • Website Overview: A demonstration of how the prototype, help section, and evaluation results are integrated into the BrainWave Diagnostics website.

6. Conclusion and Future Work

Methodology

Findings and Next Steps

The survey revealed strong user satisfaction with navigation, visual appeal, and interactivity. However, areas for improvement were identified, including the help section, report-sharing features, and ongoing monitoring functionalities.

Planned Enhancements

  • Refine the help section for clearer guidance.
  • Optimize the report-sharing process for improved usability.
  • Add interactive elements to enhance engagement.
